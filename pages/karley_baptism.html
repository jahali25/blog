Karley's Baptism

Sunday October 11, 2009

Karley turned 8 in August and in our church we bless our babies when they are infant and then baptise them when they turn 8. Karley was baptized on September 19. She was so happy and had a beautiful baptism by her father. The night before her baptism a dear friend of mine took pictures of her outside of the Temple here in San Antonio. We are so blessed to have a Temple so close. That photo shoot of Karley really shows her personality. Karely has such a sweet spirit and we absolutely love the joy she brings to our family. We are very proud of her for choosing the become a baptized member of our church. It is times like these when we really miss having extended family close. It feels very lonely to go through these wonderful experiences without them.

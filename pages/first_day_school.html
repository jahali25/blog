First Day of School

Monday August 25, 2008

What does the first day of school mean? FREEDOM for mom. Ok, I was a tiny, tiny bit sad to see Ashley off to Kinder and Kinamo off the Middle school. But mostly I was just so happy that they are back in school. The boredom and fighting last week was enough to say all of us were ready for school. I does feel weird to only have one child at home. It has been nine years since I have only had one child at home.
